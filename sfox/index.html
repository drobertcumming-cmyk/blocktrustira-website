How It Works

Animus Decides. sFOX Executes.

The relationship between Animus AI and sFOX is straightforward. Animus analyzes market data and generates trade signals. sFOX receives those signals and executes trades at institutional rates.

Your assets never leave qualified custody. Trades happen within the sFOX infrastructure — there is no transfer between platforms, no withdrawal to an external wallet, and no exposure to unregulated exchanges.

The entire loop — analysis, decision, execution, and custody — operates within a regulated, insured, and audited environment.
