How Our AI Manages Your Retirement Portfolio

The Technology

What Is Animus AI?

Animus AI was founded at the University of Southern California. It was built for one purpose: data-driven portfolio management. The system removes emotion from retirement investing.

Traditional advisors rely on intuition and experience. Animus relies on data pipelines, quantitative signals, and systematic rules. It does not panic. It does not speculate. It processes information and acts accordingly.

This is not experimental technology wrapped in marketing language. It is a production system managing real retirement capital around the clock.

The Process

How the System Works

Five stages run continuously. Each one feeds the next. The entire cycle operates without human intervention.

1

Data Collection

Automated pipelines ingest market data from multiple sources. Price feeds, volume data, and order book depth stream in continuously. The system never stops collecting.

2

Sentiment Analysis

Natural language processing scans news sources and publications. The system reads thousands of articles daily. It quantifies sentiment into actionable signals.

3

Market Trend Analysis

Quantitative models evaluate trend direction and momentum. The system identifies shifts before they become obvious. Historical patterns inform forward-looking assessments.

4

Allocation Decisions

The system generates allocation recommendations based on combined signals. Every decision follows systematic rules. Position sizes reflect current risk conditions.

5

Automatic Execution

Trades execute through sFOX, an institutional-grade exchange. Execution happens within minutes of a decision. No manual approval bottleneck. No delay.

Investment Approach

BTC and ETH. Long-Only. Non-Custodial.

The strategy focuses exclusively on Bitcoin and Ethereum. These are the two most established digital assets by market capitalization. The system does not chase speculative alternatives.

Portfolios are managed through separately managed accounts. Your assets remain in your name at all times. BlockTrustIRA does not pool client funds. Each account operates independently.

The approach is long-only. The system does not short-sell or use leverage. It manages allocation between digital assets and stable positions based on market conditions.

Risk Management

This Is Not a Prediction Engine.

"It's a risk management system."

The system does not attempt to predict prices. It manages exposure to risk. When volatility rises, it reduces position sizes. When conditions stabilize, it increases allocation methodically.

Every rule exists to protect capital first. Growth is a consequence of disciplined risk management, not aggressive speculation. The system prioritizes drawdown control above all else.
