Why We Built This

The Gap Nobody Was Filling

When we looked at the crypto IRA landscape, we saw two options — and neither one worked.

On one side, there were self-directed platforms. They offered custody and a trading interface, but no management. You were on your own. If Bitcoin dropped 20% at 2 AM, that was your problem.

On the other side, there were traditional financial advisors. They understood retirement planning but had no infrastructure for digital assets. They could not offer what they could not execute.

"The market had platforms. It had portfolio managers. It didn't have both."

BlockTrustIRA was built to close that gap. We combined Animus Technologies' AI-driven execution with sFOX's institutional custody to create something that did not exist: a fully managed crypto IRA with 24/7 active oversight.

We did not build a platform. We built a portfolio management firm — one that happens to operate in digital assets, inside tax-advantaged retirement accounts.
