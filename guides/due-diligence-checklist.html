Before You Choose a Crypto IRA: The Due Diligence Checklist

Most providers hope you will not ask hard questions. This checklist ensures you do. Know exactly what to evaluate before committing your retirement savings.

What You Will Learn

The Questions Most Investors Forget to Ask.

This checklist covers the six critical areas that separate trustworthy providers from the rest.

1. What is the custody insurance amount?

A specific dollar figure matters. Vague promises do not protect your assets.

2. Who is the qualified custodian?

Your assets should be legally separate from the platform. Verify the custodian independently.

3. What are ALL the fees?

Setup fees. Trading fees. Spread markups. Annual charges. Get the complete picture in writing.

4. Is there a lock-up period?

Some providers restrict withdrawals. Your retirement funds should remain accessible.

5. How is the portfolio managed?

Self-directed means unmanaged. Understand who or what is making allocation decisions.

6. What happens during a market crash?

Ask about risk management protocols. Silence on this topic is a red flag.
