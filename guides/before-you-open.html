What to Know Before Opening a Crypto IRA

The decisions you make before opening an account matter more than the ones after. This guide covers the fundamentals every investor should understand first.

What the Guide Covers

Six Topics That Shape Your Outcome.

Each section addresses a common knowledge gap that leads to costly mistakes.

1. Eligibility and Account Types

Traditional, Roth, SEP, and SIMPLE IRAs each have different rules. Understand which type fits your situation before you start.

2. Funding Options and Rollovers

Direct rollovers, transfers, and new contributions each have different tax implications. The wrong method can trigger penalties.

3. Tax Rules and Reporting

Crypto in an IRA follows different tax rules than a brokerage account. Learn the advantages and the obligations.

4. Custody and Security Basics

Where your crypto is held matters. Understand the difference between custodial and non-custodial arrangements.

5. Fee Structures to Evaluate

Management fees, trading fees, spread markups, and hidden charges. Know what to look for and what to question.

6. Common Mistakes to Avoid

From choosing the wrong account type to triggering taxable events. These errors are preventable with the right preparation.
