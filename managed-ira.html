The Problem

Self-Directed Means Unmanaged.

Most crypto IRA platforms call themselves "self-directed." That sounds empowering. In practice, it means you're on your own.

No one monitors your portfolio overnight. No one rebalances when markets move. No one steps in when volatility spikes at 2 AM on a Saturday. You get a login and a trading interface. Everything else is your responsibility.

For retirement savings — the money you can't afford to lose — that's not a feature. It's a liability.

"A dashboard isn't a strategy. Access isn't management."
