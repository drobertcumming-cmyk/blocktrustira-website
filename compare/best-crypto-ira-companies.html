Provider Profile

BlockTrustIRA

Overview

BlockTrustIRA is the only provider offering both self-directed and AI-managed cryptocurrency IRAs under one roof. The Managed IRA uses the Animus AI system for 24/7 portfolio monitoring and automatic rebalancing. The Self-Directed option provides a low-cost trading platform.

Fees

Managed IRA: 0.14% per trade, 2% annual management fee, 25% performance fee on profits only. Self-Directed: 0.4% per trade, no management or performance fees. No setup, monthly, or closure fees for either option.

Security

$200M custody insurance through sFOX. Institutional cold storage with multi-signature authorization. Qualified custodian structure ensures legal asset separation.

Strengths

  • Only provider with both managed and self-directed options
  • AI-driven 24/7 portfolio monitoring
  • Lowest per-trade fee in the market (0.14%)
  • Performance-aligned fee model
  • $200M specified custody insurance
  • Personal onboarding and dedicated support

Limitations

Provider Profile

iTrustCapital

Overview

iTrustCapital is a well-established self-directed crypto IRA platform. It has built a strong reputation with a clean interface and straightforward pricing. The platform supports cryptocurrency and precious metals trading within IRA accounts.

Fees

1% per trade on crypto. No monthly fees, no setup fees. The fee structure is simple and predictable. No management or performance fees since the platform is self-directed only.

Security

Uses institutional-grade custody solutions. Specific insurance amounts are not prominently published. The platform has maintained a clean security track record.

Strengths

  • 7,000+ customer reviews on Trustpilot
  • Polished mobile app (iOS and Android)
  • Clean, intuitive user interface
  • Simple, predictable fee structure
  • Strong brand recognition in the crypto IRA space
  • Supports crypto and precious metals

Limitations

  • Self-directed only — no managed option
  • 1% per-trade fee is higher than some competitors
  • Custody insurance amount not prominently specified
  • No AI monitoring or automatic rebalancing
  • You manage all investment decisions yourself
Provider Profile

BitcoinIRA

Overview

BitcoinIRA was one of the first dedicated cryptocurrency IRA platforms. It benefits from strong name recognition and early-mover advantage. The platform offers access to a wide range of cryptocurrencies.

Fees

Trading fees start at approximately 2% and may include additional spread markup. Monthly maintenance fees may apply. The fee structure is less transparent than some competitors. Setup is free.

Security

Claims $250M in custody insurance through BitGo. The platform has operated since 2016 with institutional custody infrastructure. Longer operating history provides a track record.

Strengths

  • Established brand since 2016
  • Name recognition as a pioneer in the space
  • 80+ supported cryptocurrencies
  • $250M custody insurance (specified)
  • Mobile app available
  • Interest-earning options on some assets

Limitations

  • Highest trading fees among compared providers
  • Spread markup adds hidden cost
  • $3,000 minimum investment
  • Fee structure less transparent than competitors
  • No managed portfolio option
  • Mixed customer reviews on pricing clarity
Verdict

Who Each Provider Is Best For

There is no single best crypto IRA. The right choice depends on your investment style, budget, and how much management you want.

BlockTrustIRA

Best for: Investors who want professional management or the flexibility to choose between managed and self-directed.

If you want 24/7 AI monitoring and a performance-aligned fee model, this is the only option that offers it. Also strong for self-directed investors who want the lowest per-trade fee.

iTrustCapital

Best for: Experienced self-directed investors who value a polished interface and simple pricing.

If you are confident managing your own portfolio and want a proven platform with thousands of reviews, iTrustCapital is a strong choice. The mobile app is a notable advantage.

BitcoinIRA

Best for: Investors who prioritize brand recognition and broad cryptocurrency selection.

If you want access to 80+ cryptocurrencies and prefer working with an established name, BitcoinIRA has the longest track record. Be prepared for higher fees and less pricing transparency.
