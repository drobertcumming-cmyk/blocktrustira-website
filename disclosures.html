Disclosures

Investment disclaimers, regulatory information, and fee details. Last updated: February 2026.

1. Investment Risk Disclosures

Cryptocurrency investments are volatile and carry risk, including potential loss of principal.

The value of your investment may decrease significantly. You could lose some or all of the money you invest. Only invest money you can afford to lose.

Past performance does not guarantee future results.

Historical returns, whether actual or hypothetical, are not indicative of future performance. Market conditions change. Results will vary.

BlockTrustIRA is not a financial, tax, or legal advisor.

All information provided is for educational and informational purposes only. Consult qualified professionals before making investment decisions.

Additional risks include but are not limited to:

  • Market risk: Cryptocurrency prices are highly volatile and subject to rapid, substantial declines
  • Regulatory risk: Changes in laws and regulations may adversely affect the value of digital assets
  • Technology risk: Blockchain networks and smart contracts may contain vulnerabilities
  • Liquidity risk: Certain digital assets may have limited trading volume
  • Custody risk: Despite institutional safeguards, risks related to digital asset custody cannot be fully eliminated
  • Concentration risk: A portfolio concentrated in cryptocurrency carries higher risk than a diversified portfolio

2. Fee Schedule

The following fees apply to all BlockTrustIRA managed accounts.

Fee Type Amount Details
Annual Management Fee 2% Charged on total assets under management. Assessed and deducted quarterly (0.5% per quarter).
Performance Fee 25% Charged on net profits only, above the high-water mark. If your portfolio does not grow, no performance fee is charged.
Trading Fee 0.14% Per-trade execution fee charged by sFOX. Applied to each buy and sell transaction.
Account Setup Fee $0 No charge to open your account.
Account Closure Fee $0 No charge to close your account or transfer assets.
Lock-up Period None No restrictions on withdrawals or transfers.
Hidden Fees None No spread markups, inactivity fees, or undisclosed charges.

The performance fee structure aligns our interests with yours. We earn more only when your portfolio grows. This differs from per-trade fee models where the provider profits from activity regardless of your outcome.

3. Custody and Insurance

Your cryptocurrency assets are held by sFOX, a qualified third-party custodian. Your assets are legally separate from BlockTrustIRA. Key custody details include:

  • Custody insurance: $200 million in aggregate coverage
  • Cold storage: The majority of assets are held in offline, institutional-grade cold storage
  • Multi-signature security: Multiple authorization keys are required to move assets
  • Segregated accounts: Client assets are held separately from operational funds
  • Asset separation: If BlockTrustIRA were to cease operations, your assets would remain with the custodian and accessible to you

The $200M custody insurance figure represents aggregate coverage provided by sFOX. Individual coverage limits may apply. Insurance coverage is subject to the terms and conditions of the insurance policy. Coverage does not protect against market losses.

4. Regulatory Status

BlockTrustIRA operates as a technology and advisory service facilitating cryptocurrency IRA management. We are not a bank, broker-dealer, or registered investment advisor under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940, unless otherwise disclosed.

Self-directed IRAs are facilitated through qualified custodians who maintain the necessary regulatory registrations and compliance obligations. The IRA structure is governed by IRS regulations applicable to Individual Retirement Accounts.

Cryptocurrency is not insured by the FDIC or SIPC. Digital assets are not considered securities under current SEC guidance, though this classification may change.

5. AI and Technology Disclosures

BlockTrustIRA uses proprietary artificial intelligence technology (Animus AI) for portfolio monitoring, risk assessment, and rebalancing decisions. Important disclosures about this technology:

  • AI-driven decisions are based on algorithms and data analysis, not human judgment
  • No algorithm can predict market movements with certainty
  • The AI system may make rebalancing decisions that result in losses
  • Technology systems are subject to potential outages, errors, and failures
  • Past algorithmic performance does not guarantee future results

6. Tax Considerations

Investing in cryptocurrency through an IRA may offer tax advantages, including tax-deferred or tax-free growth depending on the account type. However, tax treatment depends on your individual circumstances.

BlockTrustIRA does not provide tax advice. Consult a qualified tax professional regarding the tax implications of your investment decisions. IRA contributions, distributions, and rollovers are subject to IRS rules. Early distributions may incur penalties.
